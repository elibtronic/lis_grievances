The Grief Index

How much Grief is posted to this bot? Now you'll know.

Sometimes submission come in that can't be posted for one reason or another, this calculation shows how this trends.

Monthly GI - Calculated: (All Postable Submissions / (All Postable Submissions + Non Postable Submissions)) per month

Overall GI - Calculated: (All Postable Submissions / (All Postable Submissions + Non Postable Submissions)) for life of the bot
